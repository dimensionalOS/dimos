# VQA question bank for the *recorded-session* scenarios (china-office replay
# and full-rrd feed) — one question per line; blank lines and '#' comments are
# ignored. Unlike vqa_questions.txt (live/sim), this set avoids ego-"right
# now" questions (nearest obstacle, path clearance): against a recorded tour
# there is no meaningful current robot position.

# --- perception & catalog (item 2) ---
What did you observe?
Search for a chair and a backpack, and tell me if you found them.
How much of the space is occupied with furniture?
Which table has the most chairs?
Give me a full catalog of everything you've found, including where each item is.
Where would I be likely to find some food?
Where would I be likely to find a first aid kit?

# --- floorplan (item 1) ---
How many levels does this building have, and what does each floor's layout look like?
Generate a floor plan of this space and describe its overall shape.

# --- lidar signals (item 3): space-level queries only ---
How big is this space? Give me its rough dimensions.
How much of the space have you scanned so far?
